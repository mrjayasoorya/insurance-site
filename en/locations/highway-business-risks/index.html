Why NH belts behave differently

  • Higher incident probability + third-party involvement
  • Faster escalation (police/reporting/timelines matter)
  • Operational reality often differs from generic policy wording

Claim dispute triggers we commonly see

  • Late reporting or unclear incident timeline
  • Wording gaps for loading/unloading / route pattern
  • Missing proof trail (photos, documents, witness notes if applicable)

Do-now checklist (claim-ready habits)

  • Keep policy copy accessible
  • Basic photo routine for assets/stock
  • Maintain permits/fitness discipline for vehicles
  • Know the first 3 actions after an incident
